The short version

Who's writing this

I'm a marketing professional with over ten years of work across performance and brand campaigns. I'm currently completing a Master's in Digital Media and Global Communications at the University of Niagara Falls Canada. This site is the central artifact of my capstone (DMGC-699 Spring 2026), supervised by Dr. Wael Brahim.

The reason for the topic is plain: digital twin technology is increasingly mentioned in industry trade press, vendor decks, and consultancy reports, but it is rarely defined in a way that lets a practitioner act on it. Marketing teams in auto insurance — a sector with strong telematics and policy-personalization potential — are being asked to evaluate technology vendors without a clear practical framework. This project is one attempt to provide that framework, anchored in primary survey evidence and a working prototype.
